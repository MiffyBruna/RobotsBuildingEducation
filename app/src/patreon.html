About me

I created Robots Building Education because I believed that the future of education boils down to three key things:

  1. Every student should have access to many best-fit teachers.

  2. An inordinate amount of education and inequitable learning happens on social media. Unrecorded education or learning is a systemic failure.

  3. Learning events should build communities.

Who am I? I'm a person that moved from Chicago to the Bay Area to find work as a software engineer. I spent some time working on education and financial technology and created an audience on Tiktok by promoting hard but honest work. Now I work on my ideas with Robots Building Education.

Why do I do this? I just believe that the world should work a certain way and I want to live my truth. I feel that education technology has been generations behind other forms of media. The internet should be better. I'm not here to promise you success but I am here to try my best to provide an environment that can help you create it.
